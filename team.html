About Us

We're a group of computer science students from the University of Dayton. This website is an overview of our senior capstone project. We are working with TurtleUp, a nonprofit based in Ghana, to develop a cross-platform mobile application that will allow users to interact with the TurtleUp brand in a more unique way.

This app aims to integrate tracking features currently being developed by a team of engineering students also here at the University of Dayton. The app will pull satellite tracking data about individual turtles and display it to users in an intuitive and engaging manner.

We plan to complete a working prototype of this app by the end of the semester, as the satellite tracker is still currently in development. Once that is complete, it is likely that another group of developers will be focused on adding additional features and ensuring that our current base-design is compatible with the final version of the tracker.
